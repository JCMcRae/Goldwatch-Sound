DALLAS BRED SOUND HEARD ALL AROUND THE WORLD

Goldwatch Sound is the brainchild of music producer The Goldwatch. Hailing from Dallas, The Goldwatch plays on the diverse and robust musical culture from his experience and time growing up in the Triple D and creates music that touches the heart and soul of the listener. From classic R&B jams to the relaxing sounds of House, The Goldwatch brings a new age sound to Hip-Hop and Rap.

Like all things, The Goldwatch's craft took time to build. With roots in orchestra playing the cello, the musical and creative mindset of The Goldwatch took a change and shift when he put down the cello and turned to producing at the age of sixteen. With influences among the likes of MF Doom, Kanye West, Pharrell Williams, Cardo, and DJ Drama, The Goldwatch creates a unique sound that resembles similarities of these producers, but also a standalone sound that sets him apart from them.
